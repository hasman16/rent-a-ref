How It Works

Fill out an Online Form and we'll match you up with a Referee that fits best for your League, Tournament or Event.

Fill out the Registration Form and take a Sports Quiz to test knowledge of the Sports you're interested in Officiating.

Once your Event is confirmed by RAR, we'll send an introduction email to you and the Official.

Rent-A-Ref will send you a notification once a League, Tournament or an Event is in need of your services.

Pay the Referee, and you're ready to enjoy your event. RAR takes care of the rest so you can sit back, watch or play!

Once the event is complete, you will receive your payment via one of our many electronic payment options.
